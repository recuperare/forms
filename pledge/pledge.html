Pledge of Support

I share the belief of our founders that the powers of the federal government should be limited to those defined in the Constitution and all others should be vested in the states - and ultimately in the people.

I believe we must restore power to the hard-working taxpayers at the state and local levels so America can be more free and prosperous.

Therefore I pledge my support of Our American Revival as it works to advance policies and ideas that will reduce the size and scope of government to make it more accountable to the American people.

I authorize OAR to publicly promote my support of their principles and mission.
